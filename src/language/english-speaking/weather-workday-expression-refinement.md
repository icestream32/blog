## 🗒 今日练习回顾

这组练习主要围绕两类场景：

1. 跟别人聊天气（偏好、气候、追问天气）
2. 描述自己在工作日和周末的状态

我这次最容易出错的地方是：介词搭配、形容词语义、拼写、以及句子主干完整性。

---

### 🎯 场景 1：天气偏好表达

**原句：**
> I love the crowed weather, but the crowd weather makes me want to sleep so is not bad or not better.

**改进版：**
> I love the hot weather, but the hot weather makes me want to sleep, so it's not bad or not worse.

**解析：**
- `crowded` 常用于“拥挤的场所”，不修饰 `weather`
- 句子里需要完整主语，`so it's ...` 比 `so is ...` 更完整
- 比较语义里 `not worse` 更合理

---

### 🎯 场景 2：介绍深圳天气并继续提问

**原句：**
> I live in Shenzhen, China Sunshine is a very hott and hemi in the world so I love cloudy weather. Is the middle of the weather?

**改进版：**
> I live in Shenzhen, China. The sunshine here is very hot and humid in the world, so I love cloudy weather. What's the weather like today?

**解析：**
- 独立句要断开，避免逗号拼接
- `hott` → `hot`，`hemi` → `humid`
- 问“今天天气怎么样”优先用固定句型：`What's the weather like today?`

---

### 🎯 场景 3：周末休息 vs 工作日努力

**原句：**
> I will sleep in tired day if today is in the weekend, but in h Walker day I must work hard.

**改进版：**
> I will sleep on tiring days if today is on the weekend, but on workdays I must work hard.

**解析：**
- 时间介词固定搭配：`on the weekend`、`on workdays`
- `tired`（人感到累）与 `tiring`（事情令人累）要区分
- 泛指日子用复数：`days`
- `workdays` 是固定拼写

---

### 🎯 场景 4：表达工作日专注状态

**原句：**
> On weekdays changes staying more focused on the cloud or cover, unlike Bryan song which distracts me.

**改进版：**
> On weekdays, I try to stay more focused on work or study, unlike Bryan's songs which distract me.

**解析：**
- 原句缺主语，补上 `I`
- 动词语义修正：`changes` 改为 `try to`
- `cloud/cover` 语义模糊，替换为 `work or study`
- 所有格和复数：`Bryan's songs`

---

## ✅ 我这次沉淀下来的可复用表达

- `on the weekend / on weekends`
- `on workdays`
- `hot weather`
- `The sunshine here is very hot and humid.`
- `What's the weather like today?`

下次继续练同主题时，我会优先套这些稳定模板，再逐步做个性化扩展。
