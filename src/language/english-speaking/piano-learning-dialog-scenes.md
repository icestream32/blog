# HelloTalk 口语学习日志｜钢琴学习对话

<!-- more -->

## 🗒 今日练习回顾

今天的练习内容围绕钢琴学习展开，涵盖了音乐类型描述、初学者身份表明、学习进度汇报等话题。

### 🎵 音乐偏好交流

我用英语描述了自己喜欢的音乐类型：

**原句：**
> Sounds like a game music and anime music and the piano. Classical music makes me relax.

**改进版：**
> It sounds like game music, anime, and piano music. Classical music makes me relax.

**解析：** 补充主语 `It`，`music` 是不可数名词前面不能加 `a`，并列结构用逗号分隔更符合习惯。

---

### 🎹 表明初学者身份

我向 AI 表明自己是钢琴初学者，并请求推荐曲目：

**原句：**
> I'm the beginner of the piano, so I don't know the classical music. Can you suggest some piano music for me?

**改进版：**
> I'm a beginner at the piano, so I don't know classical music. Can you suggest some piano music for me?

**解析：** `beginner` 泛指用 `a` 不用 `the`，`classical music` 泛指不加 `the`，`at the piano` 比 `of the piano` 更地道。

---

### 📈 汇报学习进度（已完成）

我告诉 AI 最近学会的曲目：

**原句：**
> Recently I can't play the wheel songs. Now I learned and the C major for the piano.

**改进版：**
> Recently I haven't been able to play the round songs. Now I have learned "Twinkle Twinkle Little Star" and the C major scale for the piano.

**解析：** `Recently` 与现在完成时连用，`wheel songs` 应为 `round songs`（轮唱歌曲），`C major` 后补充 `scale`（音阶）。

---

### 📈 汇报学习进度（正在进行）

我说明当前正在学习的内容：

**原句：**
> Recently I haven't able to play the wheel songs. Now I learn did and the C major for the piano.

**改进版：**
> Recently I haven't been able to play the round songs. Now I am learning new pieces and C major for the piano.

**解析：** `be able to` 固定搭配，现在完成时否定形式 `haven't been able to`，正在学习用现在进行时 `am learning`。

---

### 🎼 展示学习成果

我展示自己已经学会的《小星星》：

**原句：**
> I can play the Jingle Jingle little star now and I prefer slow Chrome piece an now I learned with champ.

**改进版：**
> I can play "Twinkle Twinkle Little Star" now, and I prefer the slow piano piece that I learned with a teacher.

**解析：** 正确曲名 `Twinkle Twinkle Little Star`，`Chrome` 应为 `piano`，`champ` 应为 `teacher`。

## 📚 新词汇与用法

- **round songs** - 轮唱歌曲
- **C major scale** - C 大调音阶
- **Twinkle Twinkle Little Star** - 《小星星》
- **piece** - 乐曲、曲目
- **beginner at...** - 在...方面是初学者

这次 HelloTalk 练习让我意识到时态一致性和词汇准确性的重要性。通过 AI 的即时反馈，我能快速发现并纠正表达错误。🎹
