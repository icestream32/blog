## 🗒 今日练习回顾

今天的对话围绕「运动偏好」展开，从中学时期的团队运动聊到工作后的个人运动习惯，再到跑步地点和环境描述。整个过程涉及多个时态切换（过去时、现在时、将来时）和固定搭配的使用。

---

### 🎯 场景一：运动偏好对比

**场景对话（完整）：**

- 对方：Do you prefer team sports like basketball or individual ones like running? I love playing badminton with friends on weekends. What about you?
- 我：When I was in middle school, I preferred team sports because many people like me played volleyball, but at work I prefer individual activities like running.
- 对方：That makes sense! Team sports are fun for bonding, but running's great for unwinding after work. Do you run outdoors or on a treadmill? I find park runs way more enjoyable than gym machines.

**原句：**
> When I learning the middle school, I preferred team spot 'cause a lot of people like me will play volleyball, but in work I prefer individual ones like running.

**改进版：**
> When I was in middle school, I preferred team sports because many people like me played volleyball, but at work I prefer individual activities like running.

**解析：**
- 时态一致性：描述过去经历用过去时（was, preferred, played），描述现在偏好用现在时（prefer）
- 词汇准确性：team sports（非 team spot），at work（非 in work），because（非'cause）
- 指代明确：individual activities 比 individual ones 更具体

### 🎯 场景二：运动场所偏好

**场景对话（完整）：**

- 对方：Oh, park runs on weekends sound perfect—fresh air and nice scenery! Do you have a favorite park nearby? I always pick ones with trees; they make the run feel less tiring.
- 我：After the war, I will go to the gym, but on weekends I prefer running in the park.

**原句：**
> After war I will going to gym machines, but in weekend I preferred run running in park.

**改进版：**
> After the war, I will go to the gym, but on weekends I prefer running in the park.

**解析：**
- 定冠词：the war, the gym, the park（特指时需要加 the）
- 时态：will go（将来时，will + 动词原形），prefer（现在时描述习惯）
- 介词：on weekends（非 in weekend）
- 动词形式：prefer running（prefer doing sth.）

### 🎯 场景三：湖边环境描述

**场景对话（完整）：**

- 对方：A lake nearby sounds amazing! Running by water must be so calming. Do you ever stop to sit by the lake after your run? I'd definitely take a minute to enjoy the view if I were you.
- 我：There's a big lake near me, and I usually only check around nearby because the air is very fresh.

**原句：**
> There's a Big Lake near me and I usually round only check nearby by the air is a very fresh.

**改进版：**
> There's a big lake near me, and I usually only check around nearby because the air is very fresh.

**解析：**
- 大小写：big lake（非专有名词无需大写）
- 连词：because 表因果（非 by）
- 语序：only check around（副词 only 放在动词前）
- 标点：两个独立分句用逗号和 and 连接

### 🎯 场景四：跑步速度描述

**场景对话（完整）：**

- 我：I don't run so fast; I usually walk slowly while enjoying the scenery by the lake.

**原句：**
> I don't run so fast I usually wrong slowly while enjoying the scenery by the lake.

**改进版：**
> I don't run so fast; I usually walk slowly while enjoying the scenery by the lake.

**解析：**
- 连词使用：两个独立分句需用分号或连词连接
- 词汇选择：walk（行走）而非 wrong（错误）
- while 引导时间状语从句，表示「一边...一边...」

## 📌 今日提升与反思

| 项目 | 改进点 |
|------|--------|
| 时态一致性 | 过去经历用过去时，现在习惯用现在时，将来计划用将来时 |
| 固定搭配 | at work, on weekends, go to the gym, prefer doing sth. |
| 连词使用 | because 表因果，and 连接并列分句，分号分隔独立句子 |
| 词汇准确性 | team sports, individual activities, walk（非 wrong） |
| 冠词使用 | the war, the gym, the park（特指时加 the） |

---

## 📚 新词汇与用法

- **team sports**：团队运动（如篮球、排球）
- **individual activities**：个人活动（如跑步、游泳）
- **unwind**：放松，缓解压力
- **scenery**：风景，景色
- **bonding**：建立联系，增进感情
